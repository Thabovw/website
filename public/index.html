Biography

Dr. Thabo van Woudenberg is the research coordinator of the NWO Vici financed SocialMovez project at the Erasmus University in Rotterdam, the Netherlands. Before this postition, Thabo investigated the role of the social networks in promoting physical activity of adolescents and was part of the MyMovez research team. His work is combined in the dissertation, which he succesfully defended in Janurary 2020.

Currently, Thabo investigates how health behaviors spread online, and how online social networks can be deployed to help adolescents live a healthy life.
